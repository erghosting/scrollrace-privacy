Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ScrollRace (operated by Marco Breda, scrollrace.app@gmail.com) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our Android application.

1. Data Controller

Marco Breda
Email: scrollrace.app@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address, username, profile photo
  • Usage data: time spent on social media apps (via Android UsageStatsManager), converted to scroll distance in meters
  • Device data: anonymous device identifiers used by Firebase

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality (tracking, leaderboards, badges)
  • To send push notifications about your ranking and weekly recaps
  • To store and sync your data across sessions

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication – user login and registration
  • Cloud Firestore – storing user data and leaderboards
  • Firebase Storage – storing profile photos
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging – push notifications

These services are operated by Google LLC. See Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting scrollrace.app@gmail.com or using the Delete Account option in the app.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and export your personal data. Contact us at scrollrace.app@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

7. Children

ScrollRace is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify users of significant changes via the app.
